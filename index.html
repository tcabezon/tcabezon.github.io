I’m Tomás Cabezón Pedroso (he-him) a computational designer and engineer, born in Bilbao (Spain), currently living in Pittsburgh (US). I consider myself an interdisciplinary designer, my practice often combines both design and technology. I am currently Studying a MS in Computational Design at Carnegie Mellon University with the sponsorship of Fulbright Scholarship.

I hold a Double degree in Industrial Design and Mechanical Engineering and a Masters Degree in Computational Engineering and Mathematics. During my university years I have been an exchange student in Tongji University in Shanghai (course 2017-2018), Politecnico de Milano (fall semester 2018) and Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María de Chile (spring semester 2018).
